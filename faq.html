Frequently Asked Questions

Overview

  • What areas does Palve Ventures serve?

    We serve clients across Palghar, Boiser, Manor, Wada, Kelwa, Safale, Dhanu, Talasari, Tarapure, Vikaramgad, and Bhiwandi, offering end-to-end guidance for buying, selling, and investing in residential apartments, commercial properties, agriculture land, residential plots, industrial plots, factories, and flats.

  • Who leads Palve Ventures?

    The firm is led by Mr. Somkamt Palve, a 5 years experienced real estate professional known for transparent dealings, market expertise, and client-first advice.

  • What services do you provide?

    We handle property discovery, site visits, due diligence, pricing guidance, negotiation, documentation, and closing support for both end-users and investors.

  • How do you tailor solutions for clients?

    We start with your budget, timeline, and risk appetite, then shortlist properties that match your goals, providing transparent pros/cons, comparables, and legal checks before you decide.

  • How can I reach you quickly?

    Call 92704 49092 or email info@palveventures.com. We typically respond the same business day and can schedule site visits in Palghar and nearby areas promptly.

Costs and Payments

  • How are your consulting fees structured?

    Fees depend on the service: buyer representation, seller mandates, or investment advisory. We share a written fee quote upfront and proceed only after your approval.

  • Do you charge any hidden costs?

    No. All brokerage or consulting charges are disclosed in writing. Government duties (stamp duty, registration) and bank charges are paid directly by clients to respective authorities.

  • Which payment modes do you accept?

    We accept bank transfer, UPI, and cheque for professional fees. Property payments go directly to the seller or developer as per the agreed schedule.

  • Can you help estimate total purchase costs?

    Yes. We provide a breakup of base price, taxes, stamp duty, registration, maintenance deposits, parking, and any applicable club or development charges before you decide.

  • Do you assist with home loans?

    We can connect you with lending partners and help with documentation, eligibility checks, and property papers required for sanction.

  • What if a deal does not close?

    If a transaction falls through due to title or compliance issues uncovered during diligence, we pause and realign options. Fees are charged only as per the agreed milestones.

Safety and Security

  • How do you verify property titles?

    We review title chains, encumbrance certificates, mutation records, and approvals. For new projects, we check RERA registration, layout sanctions, and builder compliance before recommending.

  • Do you assist with legal and technical due diligence?

    Yes. We can coordinate with empaneled lawyers and engineers for document vetting, site measurements, and compliance checks so you move ahead with clarity.

  • How do you handle payments securely?

    Payments are made directly to sellers or developers via traceable modes. We align payment milestones with verified project progress or agreed sale agreements.

  • What precautions do you take for site visits?

    We schedule daylight visits, share location pins, and ensure a team representative is present. For under-construction sites, we check access permissions and basic safety readiness.

  • How is my personal data treated?

    We use your contact details only for property coordination and do not sell or share them with third parties without consent, except where required for banking or legal processing you approve.

  • How can I report concerns or fraud?

    Call 92704 49092 or email info@palveventures.com with details. We will pause the engagement, review documents, and advise next steps or alternate options.
