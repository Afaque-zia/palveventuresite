About Palve Ventures

Palve Ventures is a well-established and trusted real estate partner based in Palghar, offering comprehensive property solutions across Palghar, Boiser, Manor, Wada, Kelwa, Safale, Dhanu, Talasari, Tarapure, Vikaramgad, and Bhiwandi. With 5 years of experience under the leadership of Mr. Somkamt Palve, we specialize in residential apartments, commercial properties, agriculture land, residential plots, industrial plots, factories (for sale and rent), flats for rent, and land parcels. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction makes us a dependable name in the real estate industry.
